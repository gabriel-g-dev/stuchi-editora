Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and the security of your data. Understand how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Stuchi Editora collects only the information necessary to operate our business and improve your experience. This includes information actively provided by you in the site's forms (name, email, phone, and details about your works) and anonymized browsing data generated by the basic use of our portal to better understand our audience.

2. How We Use This Data

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your contact messages and editorial submissions.
  • Send communications about releases, anthologies, and events (if you have subscribed to our waitlist).
  • Improve the browsing experience through the strictly necessary use of cookies.

3. Sharing and Security

Stuchi Editora does not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data under any circumstances to third parties for marketing or data mining purposes. We guarantee the use of industry-standard encryption and protection best practices.

4. Your Rights

You have the guaranteed right to request Stuchi Editora to review, modify, or completely delete your data from our databases at any time. To do this, simply contact us via email at Contatos@stuchieditora.com.
