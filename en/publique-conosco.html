What do we evaluate?

  • Originality & Voice Your ability to tell a unique story.
  • Commitment Willingness to rewrite and learn.
  • Long-Term Vision Authors who want to last in the market.

Who are we looking for?

  • New Talents Unpublished authors with great narrative potential.
  • Writers in Transition Those who have self-published and are looking for a publishing house.
  • Specialists Professionals who wish to transform knowledge into a book.

What we DO NOT publish:

  • ✕ Works generated 100% by AI without curation
  • ✕ Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • ✕ Authors looking for "magic formulas" for success

Why do we select?

We believe that publishing a bad book harms the author and the market. Our filter exists to ensure that, when carrying the Stuchi stamp, your work is synonymous with quality. If you are looking for a print shop that just prints, we are not the right place. If you are looking for a Publisher, welcome.
